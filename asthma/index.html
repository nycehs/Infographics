Bad housing conditions can make asthma worse

Environmental factors can trigger asthma - but while many people assume that "environmental factors" means outdoor air quality (pollution), the real danger may be lurking closer to home. Housing conditions are a common trigger for asthma, disproportionately affecting children in low-income neighborhoods, leading to a severe health disparity.
